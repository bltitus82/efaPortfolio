About Me

I'm a web developer, digital marketing pro, and communications specialist. My specialty is creating unique solutions to challenges and approaching problems with a fresh perspective and an open mind.

I have over a decade of experience as a writer, copy-editor, and marketing professional. I've provided SEO services to e-commerce sites, SaaS platforms, and B2C & B2B companies; copy-writing and copy-editing for books, blogs, and web-based content for clients large and small; and served as a technical writer for enterprise clients, defense contractors, and other corporate clients.

In 2021, I started studying Web Development at Eleven Fifty Academy, learning the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I subscribe to the maxim that "Design is how it works", so my focus is always with the end-user in mind. I believe that technical problems and solutions can be expressed simply and elegantly, and that great development is like great writing - accessible to the audience. I love to learn, and I hope to bring my skills and passion to your team.
